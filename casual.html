User Group 1: Casual

Description

This type of user are the most common. as "casual" suggests, they are the user group with most flexibility and mobility. Their need to watch a film is constantly changing. For example, a casual film viewer may want to go ahead and watch a film after 6 month, or he/she may feel like watching more films since it is the time when he/she is very available for all sorts of activities, and watching film is only one of them. In addition, their expectation for the movie website can be lower, since they are not frequent users.

Pre-Interview Assumptions

I am expecting the group of user are as "casual" about the website as they watch the film. They may care about some basic elements on the website such as film types, time on screen, and price, but less about special offers, membership and VIP experience, because again, they are not frequent users. However, I think they are actually the majority of the users who are going to use the website, since there is huge amount of them. In conclusion, their feedback can be very important in developing a good website.
