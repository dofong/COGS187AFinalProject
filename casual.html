User Group 1: Casual

Description

This type of user are the most common. As "casual" suggests, they are the user group with most flexibility and mobility. Their need to watch a film is constantly changing. For example, a casual film viewer may want to watch a film after 6 months, or he/she may feel like watching more films during certain perdo of time since it is the time when he/she is free for all sorts of activities, and watching film is only one of them. In addition, their expectation for the movie website can be lower than hardcore viewers, but there are many more of them than hardcore viewers.

Pre-Interview Assumptions

I am expecting the group of user are as "casual" about the website as they watch the film. They may care about some basic elements on the website such as film types, time on screen, and price, but less about special offers, membership and VIP experience, because again, they are not frequent users. However, I think they are actually the majority of the users who are going to use the website, since there is huge amount of them. As a result, their feedback can be very important in developing a good website.

Post-Interview Reflections

It turns out that casual users might be “casual” about films, but they can be very critical about their general and some specific experience with the website. They care about how easy they can find information, how convenient is the checkout process, how clear is the navigation bar. Specifically, some casual viewers care about the brand of the theater: what is the style of the website, what are the colors, how do they make them feel. Also they will appreciate some user-friendly designs such as beautiful seats selection window, clear calendar of showtime etc. It turns out all of that really effect their mood to keep exploring the website or even their decision on watching movie in that particular theater. When they run out of patience to try to find the information they want, they may click away for something else. Therefore, the interview of them do provide important information on how are we going to develop the website to make them feel the pleasure.
