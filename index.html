Welcome! We are group 6, working on an independent movie theater website.

Our user groups are defined as casual movie viewers who only goes to a movie theater once a while, hardcore movie lovers who made the movie theater their second home, and vocational related persons including job seekers and current employees of the movie theater.

We have conducted interviews, provided personal thoughts and scenarios for each of the user group, as well as 6 similar sites and a complete task division list.

Please seat back and enjoy the show!
