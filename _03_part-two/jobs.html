Diversity. Oh Yeah.

We are only about diversity. We like cats and dogs. We enjoy meeting people who enjoy yoga, and meeting people who just don't enjoy yoga. In fact, we really do accept the people who are just on the fence when it comes to yoga. And if you're neither on the fence about yoga nor have a passion for or against it, then we still accept you. That's who we are. We're diverse.
